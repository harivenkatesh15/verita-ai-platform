About MedAI

MedAI is a RAG-powered general health Q&A platform built for a medical hackathon. It delivers evidence-based health information through a conversational chatbot backed by a medical knowledge base indexed on Pinecone, with Llama 3.3 70B running on Groq for ultra-fast inference.

Llama 3.3 70B (Groq)
LangChain RAG
Pinecone vector DB
HuggingFace embeddings
